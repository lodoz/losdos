#COVID19

Los Dos will be open on Friday, May 1st. Due to limited product availabilities and changing delivery schedules, we will be doing a temporary menu for the next couple weeks. We will also have temporarily hours Tuesday-Saturday 11:30-6:30, closed Sundays and Mondays.

We will have curbside pick-up and will only take orders over the phone, so please call ahead.

GIVE US A HAND BY HAVING

  1. Your order & credit card ready over the phone.
  2. The make, color and model of vehicle being used for pickup.

As we attempt to navigate a completely new business model of curbside pick-up only we ask that you please be patient with us. We will do our best to make this temporary transition as painless as possible.
